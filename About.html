About:

Connor Alan

Growing up in West Yorkshire, Connor is a third year student at University; with a background in videography he found his passion for photography in 2022 after photographing a rave. Eventually finding an interest in street photography in the Leeds area. Now Connor uses his photography to express his view of the world and capturing life as it happens in his own unique way.

As featured on Ilfords Friday Favourites #Funonfilm2023.

Currently Connor is working on his first Zine known as 'The Life Of The Mind.' set for a 2024 release. You can find updates on his zine and see his latest photography on X (Formerly Twitter) and Instagram.
